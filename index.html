About Us

Pilosen (K) Ltd has been operational in Kenya since 2008. We are situated at Ramco Court along Mombasa Road Nairobi with branches in Mombasa at Social Security House, Moi Avenue and Kisumu at KCB Building.
We are licensed by Pest Control Products Board of Kenya. We deliver comprehensive pest management solutions. We offer pest management solutions, sanitation, in-depth safety inspections and technical support to commercial, residential and hospitality areas.
Our reputation has been built over many years on the principles of reliability, credibility, care, integrity and quality.

Our Core Value

Provision of affordable, quality & prompt services to our clients by continuously identifying their needs, delivering prompt and quality services that add value

Vision

To be the 'Go-To' pest control service company for pest management solutions, providing highest quality services and value addition to our clients

Mission

To control pests through effective, eco friendly pest control management solutions for commercial and residential areas.
